Gray Cable Knit Zip-up

This cable knit sweater has become a staple in my wardrobe. It is simple but still very interesting. I used a medium weight acrylic yarn with size 7 needles.

This was my first time making a hood for a sweater. It took me a few tries to make something I was happy with. I took measurements from an existing hoodie I had to see how big to make the hood. However, once as I made the hood and sewed it onto the sweater, it was too heavy and made the sweater droop down. I ended up remaking it to be much smaller. It still fits fine and it was able to get rid of enough extra weight that it solved the drooping problem.

It was also my first time sewing a zipper onto a knitted garment. When I've put zippers onto items that I've sewn, I've always used my sewing machine. However, I was nervous to put my knitted sweater through my sewing machine, so I had to hand stitch it in. I tried to “stitch in the ditch” or sew in the lowest parts of the knit stitches to hide the thread as much as possible.

This is a portfolio project for the Fall 2022 semester of Web Design.
